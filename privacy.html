Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  FIX-Marketing

Overview

JapanCalc ("the App") is a travel utility app designed to help visitors in Japan manage currency conversion, tax-free shopping, train guidance, and trip journaling. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We believe in radical transparency: JapanCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers. Your data stays on your device.

Data We Access

LOCATION
GPS / Location Data

Used to detect your arrival in Japan and record places you visit during your trip. Location is only accessed when the app is open and only when you grant permission. Location data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

LOCAL STORAGE
Expense & Trip Data

Expenses, categories, amounts, and trip journal entries you create are stored locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

NETWORK
Exchange Rate Data

The app retrieves live currency exchange rates from a public API to provide accurate JPY conversions. No personal information is included in these requests.

AI SERVICE
Travel Story Generation (Gemini AI)

When you choose to generate a trip story, anonymized expense categories and location names (e.g., "Tokyo", "Shopping") are sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a personalized narrative. No names, account details, or personally identifiable information are included. This feature is entirely opt-in.

GEOCODING
Location Name Lookup

GPS coordinates are converted to place names (e.g., "Asakusa", "Shinjuku") using the device's built-in geocoding service. This is processed on-device or via Google's standard geocoding service and does not include any personal information.

Data We Do NOT Collect

We never collect:
  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Payment or financial credentials
  • Photos, contacts, or other device content
  • Browsing history or behavior analytics
  • Any data without your explicit action

Third-Party Services

JapanCalc uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Gemini API — AI story generation  Google Privacy Policy →
  • Google Geocoding — Location name lookup  Google Privacy Policy →
  • Exchange Rate API — Live currency data (no personal data sent)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

Data Retention & Deletion

All trip data (expenses, locations, stories) is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time by:

  • Using the "Clear all" button within the My Trip tab
  • Uninstalling the app, which removes all locally stored data

We do not retain any data on our servers because we do not collect it in the first place.

Children's Privacy

JapanCalc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us so we can address it promptly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
