Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE: JUNE 9, 2026 · FIX-MARKETING

Overview

JapanCalc is designed to be private by default. The app requires no account, and we do not collect or store your personal information on our servers. Your trip data — expenses, locations, and travel logs — stays on your device.

JapanCalc does use a small number of third-party services to function: Google Play (distribution and purchase), Google AI / Gemini (AI-generated content), an exchange rate API, and affiliate partners. Each is described below.

Data We Access

  • LOCATIONGPS / Location Data

    Used to log the places you visit for your travel diary (MY TRIP / Farewell). Location data is processed and stored only on your device. It is never uploaded to our servers.

  • LOCAL STORAGEExpense & Trip Data

    Your spending records, split-bill history, and trip logs are stored locally on your device. Deleting the app deletes this data.

  • NETWORKExchange Rate API

    The app fetches currency rates from a public exchange rate API. These requests contain no personal information — only the currency pair being requested.

  • NETWORKAI / Gemini API

    To generate your Morning Message, Route Finder results, and Farewell story, the app sends an anonymized trip summary (e.g., area names, spending totals, day counts) to Google's Gemini API. No name, email, device ID, or precise GPS coordinates are included.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • NONEName, email, or account information

    JapanCalc has no sign-up. We never ask for, or store, your identity.

  • NONEDevice IDs or advertising identifiers

    We do not read or transmit advertising IDs or device fingerprints.

  • NONECrash reports

    We do not run our own crash reporting. (Google Play may provide aggregate, anonymized crash statistics as part of its standard platform services.)

  • NONEPayment information

    Your purchase is handled entirely by Google Play. We never see your card number or billing details.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Play

    Distribution and one-time purchase processing. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Google AI / Gemini

    Generates the Farewell story, Morning Message, and Route Finder answers from anonymized trip summaries. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Affiliate Partners (Airalo, Wise, Klook)

    The app's Settings screen contains optional affiliate links to travel services. If you tap one, you leave the app and the partner's own privacy policy applies. JapanCalc shares no data with these partners — the links are plain outbound links.

This website (japancalc-lp.vercel.app) uses Google Tag Manager / Google Analytics to measure anonymous page traffic.

Your Rights

Because JapanCalc stores no personal data on any server, there is nothing for us to export, correct, or delete on your behalf — all of your data lives on your device. To erase it, simply delete the app.

Questions about this policy? Contact us at fujimobilelife@gmail.com or via the Support page.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Material changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
