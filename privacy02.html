Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026  ·  FIX-Marketing

Overview

JapanCalc ("the App") is a travel utility app designed to help visitors in Japan manage currency conversion, tax-free shopping, train guidance, and trip journaling. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

We believe in radical transparency: JapanCalc does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers. Your data stays on your device.

Data We Access

LOCATION

GPS / Location Data

Used to detect your arrival in Japan and record places you visit during your trip. Location data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to our servers.

LOCAL STORAGE

Expense & Trip Data

Expenses, categories, amounts, and trip journal entries you create are stored locally on your device using standard Android storage. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share it.

NETWORK

Exchange Rate API

JapanCalc fetches live exchange rates from a public API. Only a standard HTTP request is made — no personal data is sent. If offline, the last cached rate is used.

AI / GEMINI API

Trip Story Generation

If you use the Farewell feature, your anonymized trip summary (dates, places, spending totals) is sent to Google's Gemini API to generate a travel story. No name, email, or device ID is included.

Data We Do Not Collect

JapanCalc does not collect:

✕  Name, email address, or any account information

✕  Device ID or advertising identifiers

✕  Crash reports or analytics (beyond what Google Play provides)

✕  Payment information (handled entirely by Google Play)

Third-Party Services

GOOGLE PLAY

Purchase & Distribution

JapanCalc is distributed via Google Play. Google's privacy policy governs the purchase process. We do not receive your payment details.

GEMINI API

Google AI (Optional)

Used only for the Farewell trip story feature. Data sent is limited to anonymized trip summaries. See Google AI Terms.

Your Rights

Since JapanCalc does not collect personal data, there is nothing to request deletion of from our servers. All your trip data is stored on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

For any questions, contact us at support.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of JapanCalc constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
