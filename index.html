HOW TO RIDE

Japan's trains
are genuinely hard.

Multiple payment systems. Unstaffed gates. Rush-hour crowds. No one to ask for help in English.

🚃
IC card, paper ticket, or credit card? Every gate type accepts different payments. There's no sign explaining which to use — you're expected to already know.
👤
Staff are rare — and rarely speak English Most gates run fully automated. The one person nearby is often as confused by your question as you are.
⏱️
Rush hour waits for no one Tokyo processes millions of commuters daily. A red light at the gate is a very public moment of pressure.
📵
Google Maps won't save you Which card works here? How much to top up? Where's the recharge machine? These answers aren't on any map.

JapanCalc's HOW TO RIDE tab was built for exactly this moment.
