How to use JapanCalc

01
Download & Open

Get JapanCalc on Google Play and open it. No account, no sign-up — you're ready in seconds.

02
PRICE — Currency Conversion

Type any yen amount and see it instantly in your home currency. Rates refresh automatically whenever you're online.

03
SPLIT — Split Bills

Divide a bill among any number of people. Japan has no tipping culture, so an even split is all you need.

04
TAX FREE — Refund Checker

Spend ¥5,000 or more and you may qualify for the 10% consumption tax refund. Check your eligibility on the spot, before you reach the counter.

05
HOW TO RIDE — Train Guide

IC card (Suica / PASMO) guides, Shinkansen booking and boarding tips, the AI Route Finder, transfer guides, and Japanese train etiquette — all in one tab.

06
MY TRIP — Travel Log

Your daily spending is logged automatically, with an AI morning message each day. When your trip ends, JapanCalc writes your Farewell story. All data stays on your device.
