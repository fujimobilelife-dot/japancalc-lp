How to use JapanCalc

01
Download & Open

Get JapanCalc on Google Play and open it. No account, no sign-up — you're ready in seconds.

02
PRICE — Currency Conversion

Type any yen amount and see it instantly in your home currency, with one-tap switching between Shopping 10% and Take-out 8% tax. Works offline; rates refresh automatically whenever you're online.

03
ASK — Ask Anything About Japan

Type any question — "I'm at Nagoya Station, where's good ramen near me?" — and AI answers with specifics for where you're standing. Tipping, tattoos, etiquette: ask away.

04
HOW TO RIDE — Train Rescue

Four sub-features in one tab: the AI Route Finder between any two stations, 🆘 Trouble? for instant answers at the ticket gate, IC Card (Suica / PASMO) guides, and Transfer help.

05
MY TRIP — Your 30 Days

A Day X / 30 countdown, plus a feeling stamp and a short note each day. On your trip's final day, tap "Your Japan Story" and AI turns everything you kept into one story. Your diary stays on your device.
